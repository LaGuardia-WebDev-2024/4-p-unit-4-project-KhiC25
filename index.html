Band Is A Sport

Welcome to my Page. Where we talk about this contreversal topic of whether band is a true sport or not.

Being In Band Takes A Lot of:

  • Time
  • Dedication
  • Practice
  • Professionalism
  • Consistency
  • EXERCISE/CONDITIONING

Musicians in band spend all summer; conditioning, and practicing music. They condition their bodies to be ready for very long parades filled with non-stop playing. To do this they work on breathing teqniques for horn players, marching techniques, build stamina by running, and help tone the body by doinmg regular workouts like pushups, situps, jumoing jacks, etc.

Common Summer Band Camp Exercises

Exercise Amount (sets)
Pushups 25 (5)
Situps 20 (5)
Jumping Jacks 25 (5)
Mountain Climbers 15 (3)

This is just a portion of the work band members have to do to get their bodies ready for a long season These are commonly used exercises that strengthen necessary muscles like shoulders, upper body, abs, and calfs/legs.
